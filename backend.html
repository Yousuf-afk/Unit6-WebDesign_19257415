Junior Green IT Developer

Description

We're looking for a young passionate users who are rearing to go learn efficient coding and simple coding, To help the environment and save our planet. You need to be passionate in our planet and business.

What you will be doing

  • Building and maintaining robust APIs for our frontend applications.
  • Optimizing server-side logic for maximum speed and scalability.
  • Implementing security and data protection settings.

Qualifications

  • Strong knowledge of Node.js, Python, or Go.
  • Experience with SQL (PostgreSQL) and NoSQL (MongoDB) databases.
  • Familiarity with cloud platforms like AWS or Google Cloud.
  • Understanding of RESTful APIs and microservices.
