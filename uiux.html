UI/UX Designer

Description

Hot Beans is looking for a UI/UX Designer who lives at the intersection of beauty and usability. You will be responsible for turning complex problems into intuitive, pixel-perfect digital experiences.

What you will be doing

  • Designing high-fidelity wireframes and interactive prototypes.
  • Conducting user research and testing to validate design decisions.
  • Collaborating with developers to ensure the final product matches the vision.
  • Maintaining and evolving our internal design system and branding.

Qualifications

  • A strong portfolio showcasing web and mobile design projects.
  • Expert-level knowledge of Figma or Adobe XD.
  • Understanding of user-centered design principles and accessibility.
  • Basic knowledge of HTML/CSS is a plus (to help speak the developers' language!).

Courses

  • Links to courses to learn more about being a UI/UX Designer: IDEOU, Learn to Become a UI/UX Designer

Pay

£40,000 - £55,000 / year

Hours

35 Hours Per Week
